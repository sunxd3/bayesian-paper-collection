# SARS-CoV-2 Household Transmission

Recursive combinatorial household final-size model with P-spline time-varying introduction hazard.

## Citation

van Boven M, van Dorp CH, et al. (2024). Estimation of introduction and transmission rates of SARS-CoV-2 in a prospective household study. *PLOS Computational Biology* 20(1): e1011832.

## Links

- **Paper**: https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pcbi.1011832
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/mvboven/sars2-households

## Metadata

- **PPL**: Stan (CmdStanR)
- **Language**: R
- **Published**: 2024
- **Scale**: 307 households, 59 outbreaks, 594-line monolithic Stan model, 8 model variants
